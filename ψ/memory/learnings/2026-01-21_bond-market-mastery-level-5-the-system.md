# ## Bond Market Mastery - Level 5: The System

## Bond Market Mastery - Level 5: The System

### The Debt Cycle (Understanding the Trap)
```
1. Government needs money
2. Issues Bonds (borrows)
3. Must pay interest
4. Needs more money to pay interest
5. Issues MORE Bonds
6. Supply increases → Prices fall → Yields rise
7. Interest payments grow
8. Eventually: Print money OR Default
```

### US Debt Reality (2026)
- National Debt: $36+ Trillion
- Annual Interest: ~$1 Trillion (exceeds defense spending)
- Debt/GDP: >100% (highest since WWII)

### The Trap
- Can't raise rates too much → interest payments explode
- Can't cut rates too much → inflation returns
- Can't print too much → currency devalues
- Can't default → global chaos
- TRAPPED.

### Bond Vigilantes
Investors who sell bonds to protest bad fiscal policy:
- Force yields higher
- Make government borrowing expensive
- Currently ACTIVE (Japan 40Y record, US 30Y near 5%)
